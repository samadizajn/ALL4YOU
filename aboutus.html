About us

ALL4YOU Overseas Education Consulting, providing consulting services related to training opportunities abroad, is a well-established organization. Our organization is the official representative of higher education institutions providing education in English in Hungary, the Czech Republic, Slovakia, Bulgaria, Croatia, Romania and Serbia, as well as Austrian and German universities.

ALL4YOU Overseas Education Consultancy is a dynamic family institution with its long standing experience in Hungary, Slovakia, the Czech Republic, Bulgaria, Latvia, Lithuania, Estonia, Austria, Germany, Croatia, Romania and Serbia and its aim is to provide you reliable support. It is our priority to please the students who will be trained in the countries in Europe that we have close heart and blood ties.
